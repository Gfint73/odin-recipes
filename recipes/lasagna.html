Ingredients

  • Sheet pasta
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef or baked beans
  • Extra cheese
  • A third helping of cheese
  • Marinara

Cooking Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef/baked beans.
  2. Place a layer of sheet pasta in a baking pan.
  3. Add a layer of cheese.
  4. Add a layer of marinara.
  5. Add ground beef and diced tomatoes.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5.
  7. Finish off the lasagna with a final layer of sheet pasta.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes at 350F.
  9. Enjoy!
